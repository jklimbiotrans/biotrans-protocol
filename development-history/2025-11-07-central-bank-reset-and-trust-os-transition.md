## 0. Overview — Why a “Trust OS” Became Necessary

From the 2008 financial crisis onward, the global economy relied on:
- **Monetary expansion (QE)** by central banks,  
- Asset price inflation (real estate, stocks, gold), and  
- **Relative decline of labor and youth income.**

By the mid-2020s, a collective realization emerged:

> “I work hard, yet I cannot afford a home.  
> Inherited wealth is now more powerful than human effort.”

This psychological exhaustion marks the historical moment when the **Biotrans Protocol** was designed — as an ethical engine experimenting with **trust beyond currency.**

## 1. Background: 2008–2025 — The Era of Monetary Expansion

### 1.1 2008–2020: The QE Foundation
- Following the Lehman collapse, the U.S., EU, and Japan printed unprecedented liquidity.
- QE prevented deflation but created a new hierarchy:
  - Asset owners grew richer.  
  - Wage earners lost purchasing power.  
  - “Money works harder than humans” became the global paradox.

### 1.2 2020–2025: Pandemic, Inflation, and Trust Erosion
- Pandemic stimulus accelerated money supply even further.  
- Real inflation — food, rent, and energy — hit ordinary households first.  
- The public narrative shifted:
  > “Central banks protect markets, not people.”  
- Inflation became not just economic, but **ethical disillusionment.**

## 5. From Currency Issuance to Decentralized Credit Creation

### 5.1 Centralized Model
- Central bank issues base money;  
- Commercial banks issue credit using **collateralized assets** (mostly real estate).  
> “Credit = a legal promise secured by collateral and state enforcement.”

### 5.2 Silicon Valley Exception — Collective Reputation Collateral
Venture capital (e.g., Sequoia, a16z, YC) treats **network reputation** as collateral.  
Belonging to the “trust web” (Stanford–MIT–YC) substitutes for tangible assets.

> For the elite, *network = collateral.*  
> For everyone else, *property = collateral.*

### 5.3 Decentralized Model (DAO)
DAO-based credit relies on:
- **Digital asset collateral** (ETH, BTC → DAI)  
- **Behavioral reputation** (SBT, MeritSBT, contribution history)  
- **Collective collateral** (treasury-backed or cooperative guarantees)

| Model | Trust Basis | Example |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| Central Bank | State + Legal Collateral | Fiat system |
| Silicon Valley | Network + Tech Credibility | Sequoia, YC |
| DAO | On-chain Assets + Reputation | MakerDAO, Gitcoin |
| **Biotrans** | **Conscience + Moral Resonance** | MeritSBT, Ethical OS |

> “Credit will evolve from collateralized assets  
> to **verified conscience** — a measurable echo of trust.”
