*(Indirect observable signals of emotional neurostates)*

The system does not measure hormones directly.  
Instead, it uses **behavior-based emotional indicators** that reliably correspond  
to underlying serotonin, dopamine, and cortisol patterns.

### The five core indicators:

1. **Long-term repetition**  
   - reliable marker of meaning-driven behavior (serotonin)

2. **Resonance diversity**  
   - multiple unrelated individuals affirm the same good act

3. **Propagation / contagion**  
   - one act inspires others (dopamine → serotonin transition)

4. **Self-replication (repentance loop)**  
   - continuous moral improvement over time

5. **Stress spikes**  
   - signs of judgment, coercion, hostility (cortisol pattern)

These indicators allow the Protocol to interpret human emotional behavior  
without quantifying biological hormones directly.
